Festival Applications

Apply to join the Marvel City Caribbean Festival marketplace as a vendor, or reach out to register your band or march group for the parade.

Vendor application

Reserve booth space for food, retail, or services at the festival marketplace. Download the application, complete every section, and submit it using the instructions printed on the form (mail, email, or drop-off as directed).

Keep a copy for your records. If you have questions before you submit, use Contact and mention Vendor application in your message.

Download 2026 vendor application (PDF)

Parade band registration

March with your band or organized group in the official festival parade. Tell us your group name, approximate roster size, and the best phone number or email to reach you.

We will reply with registration steps, lineup details, and any fees or forms required for your group.
