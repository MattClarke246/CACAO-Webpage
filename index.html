Our Mission

The Central Alabama Caribbean American Organizations (CACAO) is dedicated to preserving and sharing the rich heritage, traditions, and vibrant culture of the Caribbean islands within our local community.


Through community events, cultural showcases, and our primary initiative—raising funds to provide higher education scholarships for Caribbean American students—we strive to build a legacy of strength, unity, and excellence.

Scholarship Program

We believe education is the foundation of a thriving community. Every year, we sponsor students of Caribbean descent to help them achieve their collegiate dreams.
